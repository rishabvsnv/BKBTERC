WHO WE ARE

Welcome To Balkavi Bairagi Teacher Education Research Centre

Balkavi Bairagi Teacher Education Research Centre (BKBTERC) is a leading teacher education institution located in Suvakheda, Neemuch, Madhya Pradesh.

The institution is dedicated to nurturing future educators through academic excellence, innovative teaching practices, professional ethics, and practical learning experiences.

BKBTERC focuses on creating competent teachers equipped with modern educational methodologies, leadership skills, and strong academic foundations.
